XI / XII / BOARD

DCI offers tuition classes to the students of classes XI, XII, with the aim of preparing the students for competitive exams like IIT – JEE, KCET and NEET.

DCI caters to the needs of students from classes XI and XII who are studying in CBSE, ICSE,Bihar State Board. It also provides Foundation course for IIT – JEE Mains and NEET – UG medical exams.

DCI is one of the best coaching centres in Darbhanga for XI. XII . Our study programs are designed and delivered by highly qualified and experienced professionals from the field of education. Our mission is to create an excellent environment for students to enjoy the process of learning in their pursuit of achieving excellence.

We cover all the subjects in class XI and XII such as Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computer Science and Economics.

What we offer?

  2. Classroom coaching program
  3. Long, Medium and Short-Term courses available
