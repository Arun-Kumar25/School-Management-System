ABOUT SCHOOL

Army Public School Kapurthala is situated in the erstwhile princely state of Kapurthala, Punjab. Army Public School, Kapurthala traces its roots back to the year 1992. Inaugurated by Brig Vinod Suri Cdr 23 (1) Armd Bde the then chairman of school. Over the years, the school has grown into a full-fledged Senior Secondary School, comprising of Commerce and Humanities streams. The school being coeducational provides a holistic approach to children.
